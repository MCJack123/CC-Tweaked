The cc.http.gist module allows programs to easily download and upload Gists.
Once loaded, these are the functions that will be available:
gist.get(id[, progress])
gist.getAll(id[, progress])
gist.run(id[, progress], ...)
gist.info(id[, progress])
gist.put(files[, description[, id[, interactive]]])
gist.delete(id[, interactive])
See the "gist" help for more information about Gist.
